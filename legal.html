Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
Shomer is built on a simple principle: protect your inbox without compromising your privacy. We process your data to keep you safe — never to profile, track, or monetize you.

1. What Data We Collect

Data processed temporarily (never stored permanently):

  • Email subject line — used for phishing analysis
  • Sender email address and domain — used to check domain reputation
  • Email body text — automatically sanitized before processing. Phone numbers, credit card numbers, ID numbers, passwords, and IP addresses are replaced with placeholders before leaving your device
  • URLs found in the email — scanned for malware and phishing
  • File hashes (SHA-256) of attachments — the actual file content is never uploaded unless VirusTotal requires it for unknown files
  • SPF and DKIM headers — read locally, never stored

Data stored locally on your device only:

  • Scan history (last 10 scans)
  • Whitelist and blacklist of domains you define
  • Sender memory
  • Scan statistics and your settings

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store email content on our servers
  • We do not store your email address or personal identity
  • We do not read emails you haven't opened
  • We do not access your Gmail credentials
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers
  • We do not track your browsing history

4. Data Retention

  • Our servers: Email content is processed in real-time and not stored. Results are cached in memory for up to 1 hour, then discarded automatically.
  • Your device: All local data can be cleared at any time from Settings → Clear all data.
  • Community Trust (Supabase): Only domain names and aggregated risk counts are stored. No personal data.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your data — visible in History and Reports
  • Delete all local data — Settings → Clear all data
  • Opt out of community trust reporting — Settings → Privacy

6. Security

  • All communication uses HTTPS
  • Personal information is sanitized on your device before transmission
  • We do not log individual scan requests or link them to user identities

7. Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@shomer.app
